How to Play

Goal

Move the pieces until the playable board exactly matches the Target Card.

Turns

Tap a piece to reveal legal empty squares, then tap a highlighted square to move. Pieces never capture.

The target tools rotate or flip the Target Card. Each target change adds one move.

Card Chain

After solving a card, you'll get a message indicating how well you did. Try to get it perfect! ..you can either retry the card or get a new target card and starting from your previously solved board, try to target the new positions.

♔

King

Moves one square in any direction.

♕

Queen

Slides horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Cannot jump over pieces.

♖

Rook

Slides horizontally or vertically. Cannot jump over pieces.

♗

Bishop

Moves diagonally only between dark squares. Cannot jump over pieces.

♘

Knight

Moves in an L shape and can jump over pieces. It can never land on the center square.
